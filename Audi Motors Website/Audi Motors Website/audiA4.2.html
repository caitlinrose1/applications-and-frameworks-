Audi A4

Prepare to be impressed by the Audi A4, a sedan that seamlessly combines style, performance, and innovation. From its striking exterior design to its meticulously crafted interior, every detail of the A4 exudes luxury and sophistication. Step inside, and you'll find yourself surrounded by premium materials and cutting-edge technology, creating a driving environment that is as comfortable as it is advanced. With its powerful engine options and responsive handling, the A4 offers a driving experience that is both exhilarating and refined, setting a new standard for what a luxury sedan can be.

Engine Size: 1800cc Nestled beneath its refined exterior, the Audi A4 features a potent 1800cc engine, delivering impressive performance with sophistication.
Engine Power: 200 bhp Unleash 200 brake horsepower with the Audi A4, offering a dynamic and luxurious driving experience, blending power with comfort.
Acceleration (0-62mph): 6.9 seconds Embark on swift journeys as the Audi A4 accelerates from 0 to 62 mph in just 6.9 seconds, showcasing its remarkable agility and responsiveness.
Top Speed: 150mph Experience the exhilaration of speed as the Audi A4 effortlessly reaches a top speed of 150 mph, elevating every drive to new heights.
Fuel Tank Capacity: 55 Litres Enhancing convenience for longer travels, the Audi A4's 55-litre fuel tank ensures ample range between refueling stops, facilitating extended journeys.
