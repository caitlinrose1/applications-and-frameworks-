Audi A1

The Audi A1, a testament to Audi's commitment to luxury and performance in a compact package, is a stylish and dynamic premium hatchback. Boasting a perfect blend of urban agility and sophisticated design, the Audi A1 makes a bold statement on the road.

Engine Size: 1200cc Under the hood, the Audi A1 houses a responsive 1200 cc engine, delivering a perfect balance of power and efficiency.
Engine Power: 110 bhp With 110 brake horsepower at your disposal, the Audi A1 ensures a spirited and enjoyable driving experience.
Acceleration (0-62mph): 9.5 seconds The Audi A1 accelerates from 0 to 62 mph in just 9.5 seconds, showcasing its quick and nimble performance.
Top Speed: 130mph Experience the thrill of reaching a top speed of 130 mph, making every drive an exhilarating journey.
Fuel Tank Capacity: 45 Litres The 45-litre fuel tank ensures ample range between refueling stops, providing convenience for longer journeys.
